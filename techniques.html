The RoundHouse Kick

At OctoMuyThai, this technique is the cornerstone of your Muy Thai training. The Muy Thai roundhouse kick is the iconic technique within the martial art and not only can cause devastating damage to opponents but with honed technique can display great poise and beauty in execution.

The Push Kick "Teep"

The pusk kick or "teep" is a key component in the Muy Thai figher's arsenal. Just like a jab, this technique is vital in gauging distance and keeping your opponent at a long range which can enable you to control and dictate the fight.

Knee Strikes

Knee strikes are a powerful close range tool. Whether they are thrown in the clinch or as part of combinations to get in close and deliver this lethal blow.

The Jab

The jab should be your best punch! This is range finder and the set up for of your other power punches and therefore perfecting your technique on this strike is vital for any fighter.

The Lead Hook

A tight lead hook is the perfect power punch to hone to ensure your opponent respects your punches. Setting this punch up with a jab or cross and getting good rotation in the hips is essential for maximising effectiveness.

Elbow Strikes

Elbows are another very powerful close range strike. These strikes are great at cutting the opponent and causing blunt trauma to your opponents!
